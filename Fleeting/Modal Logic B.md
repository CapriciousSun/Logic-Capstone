Modal logic $\textbf{B}$ refers to the Brouwerian form of modal logic.
## + Formalism +
Modal logic $\mathbf{K}$ has three basic axioms
- The necessitation rule, where $(\vDash A) \to (\vDash \Box A)$
- The distribution axiom, where $\Box(A \to B) \to (\Box A \to \Box B)$
- The reflexivity axiom, $\Box A \to A$
Modal logic $\mathbf{B}$ just adds an extra axiom
- $A \to \Box \Diamond A$
